Back on her feet, the woman turned to see another figure, far different from the ones that chased her before. She was blonde, donning a white officer’s coat with black accents, over top of dark pants with matching combat boots. Her gloved hands clutched what looked to be a rapier, but with a flat blade as opposed to the usual needle-like protrusion, in her right hand, and a 9mm pistol in her left. The edge of the blade seemed to give off a very dim blue light, despite looking like normal steel. Red eyes pierced the runner’s blue ones, and the attacker opened her mouth to speak.
