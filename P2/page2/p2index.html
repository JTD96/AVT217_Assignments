The figure was female, dressed in black clothes lined with belts and pockets. Throwing knives were stashed across her chest and at either hip, with one larger knife strapped to her right boot. In the woman’s gloved right hand held a briefcase, lined with black leather and steel along its corners. Embroidered with silver thread on the left breast of the woman’s black jacket was a fox’s head. The figure passed the briefcase to her other hand as she took off, moving away from the building and towards the forest behind it. She looked behind her once at the building, seeing it as quiet as she left it.

When she turned her head back to where she was running, multiple figures started emerging from the trees. Donned in sleek, futuristic plate armor, full face masks and hoods, each one was indistinguishable from the other. One by one, they advanced on the running woman.
