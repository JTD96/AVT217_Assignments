The Black Fox felt a hard blow to the back of her head. Her vision blacked out for a moment as she fell to the ground, landing roughly on the pavement. One of the figures that chased her before stood over her, joined by the woman in white.

“Thanks for the assist,” the woman in white uttered to the armored figure. “I’ll take things a little more seriously next time. As for you, Miss Black Fox…at least take pride in the fact that it took the Lead Enforcer of the White Sector to finally bring you in. Should be enough…right?”

The woman in white brought her heel down on the thief’s head.
